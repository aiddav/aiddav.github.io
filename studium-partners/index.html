Studium Partners

International expansion for UK schools

Many UK schools are reassessing international expansion as a way to protect and grow income.

We help leadership teams decide where to focus, how to choose partners, and how to protect governance and brand over time.

What We Do

We advise senior leadership teams and boards on international expansion strategy and execution.

Our work typically results in:

  • a prioritised set of target markets
  • a clear partner model: franchise, joint venture, or operator-led
  • defined governance structures to protect academic standards and brand
  • a board-ready recommendation with explicit risks and trade-offs
What We Are Seeing

Across the schools we speak to, three patterns are consistent:

Demand

Demand remains strong in GCC and parts of Asia, but partner quality varies significantly.

Governance

Governance is often under-specified early, creating issues later.

Complexity

Expansion decisions are frequently made without a clear view of long-term operating complexity.

We focus on helping schools avoid these failure modes.

How We Work

We run a focused 6-8 week process, working directly with senior leadership.
