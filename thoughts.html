Thoughts

Does GenAI actually increase productivity for developers? And what do they do with the saved time?

Previous studies have shown up to a 55% productivity increase for developers using AI tools but this hasn't been replicated in a UK field setting.

What actually causes inflation?

Friedman argued that as inflation expectations adjust to higher inflation outcomes, inflation reduction policies in the long-run will only result in higher inflation and no benefit in terms of unemployment. So how important is expectation management in controlling inflation?

What causes wages to go up or down?

The neo-classical approach simply suggests that wages are a function of labour supply and demand. In reality, labour market inefficiencies exist e.g. discrimination, working hour constraints and impact wage differentials
